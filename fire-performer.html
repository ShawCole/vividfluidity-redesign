About Ashley's Fire Performance Career

Early 2014 Ashley met Rockfire In Bermuda. One of the members and her found, that they had tumbling in command. It was an interesting group of people, with interesting equipment on a beautiful beach. The art form is called Flow Arts. Ashley became quickly very fond of Flow Arts. She enjoys mostly all props related to flow art. Which basically is an art form that integrates dance and prop manipulation.

Ashley was welcomed into the Rockfire family, fast. That summer she performed all over the island. As a 17 year old it was amazing to do what she loved, and get paid. She could practice, show up, wow people, and sometimes even stay, and have fun, at bermudiful events.

After that summer Ashley left Bermuda to study finance for 3 years. She was a creative artist, but she had to understand the financial part of companies. In Denmark she continued to play with fire, throughout the 3 years she had 6 fire gigs. This was a good side hustle along with coaching, and hired as a waitress and salesmen.

The Rockfire family had such a huge impacted on her life in the such short time she had spent with them. She even was the logo tattooed behind her neck.

Since 2018 Ashley has been managing performances. In Denmark she performers under the name Glødende Underholdning and in Bermuda for Vivid Elements.

When she goes traveling, she always try to seek out other flow artists, as they are welcoming, kind and inspirational.

"I love the Flow Arts community, and it's unifying energies".

- Ashley.
