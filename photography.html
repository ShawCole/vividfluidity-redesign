Ashley's Photography Career

Ashley inherited her passion for capturing moments from her mother, who has always had a keen interest in capturing moments. After completing three years of studying business, Ashley decided to dive into photography, despite having little knowledge about cameras. She quickly developed a deep love for the art when her studies started at Media College Denmark in 2017 and became an apprentice at Two & Quarter in Bermuda.

In 2024 Ashley completed her Photography certification and now offers global photography sessions independently and as a freelancer for other companies.
